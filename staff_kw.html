Keith Williams is a native to the Kansas City area having grown up in South Kansas City, Missouri. Always a mind for mechanical things his father and a personal friend encouraged him to pursue a degree in engineering. Keith first attended the University of Missouri in Kansas City prior leaving home for Kansas State University where he graduated in 1988 with is BS in Mechanical Engineering. (He and Warren West were in the same fraternity although a few years span between them). Keith’s first job was as a process and product engineer for Allied Signal Aerospace (Nuclear Weapons Facility). After five years in the “bomb factory”, Keith moved on to Black & Veatch where he served the Power Industry as a Quality Assurance Engineer, Lead Mechanical Engineer and Project Mechanical Engineer. Here he became a Registered Mechanical Engineer, a Certified Weld Inspector and a Certified Quality Auditor. His next adventure came based on an urge to enter the equipment sales industry where he spent 5 years with a notable sales company prior to leaving to start Associated Equipment Sales. Keith is married to Cheryl and they have four children: Max, Charlie, Henry and Isabella.
